about me

Rae Lagman was born and raised in Las Vegas, Nevada, and has always been interested in creative pursuits for as long as she can remember. Her primary interests are in illustration, concept design, and graphic design. She is currently a Graphic Design senior at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, completing a degree program that has given her a sophisticated sense of visual storytelling and problem-solving throughout the various artistic disciplines she works within.
